WHO WE ARE

A Legacy of
Innovation

Founded in 1998, Nexus Group has grown from a single technology startup into a diversified global conglomerate. We invest in, build, and operate businesses that define entire industries — from advanced semiconductor fabrication to clean energy infrastructure.

Our philosophy is simple: identify transformative opportunities, assemble world-class teams, and execute with relentless precision. The result is a portfolio of six powerhouse subsidiaries, each a leader in its domain.
